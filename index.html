Cuisines

A cuisine is a style of cooking characterized by distinctive ingredients, techniques and dishes, and usually associated with a specific culture or geographic region.

You can read more about cuisines on wikipedia. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cuisine

The following cuisines are available to choose from:

  • Asian
    Cuisines originating from asian countries such as China, Japan, Korea etc.
  • BBQ & Grill
    Any cuisine that uses a BBQ Grill.
  • Burgers
    Sandwitches that consist of a bun, meat and other ingredients.
  • Indian
    Cuisines originating in India.
  • Italian
    Cuisines originating in Italy.
  • Thai
    Cuisines originating in Thailand.
